General Anil Chauhan

Chief of Defence Staff, India

Early Life and Education

Born in Pauri Garhwal, Uttarakhand, General Anil Chauhan is an alumnus of the National Defence Academy and the Indian Military Academy.

Military Career

Commissioned into the 11 Gorkha Rifles in 1981, he has held key command and staff roles, including commanding the Eastern Command.

Honors and Awards

  • Param Vishisht Seva Medal
  • Uttam Yudh Seva Medal
  • Ati Vishisht Seva Medal
  • Sena Medal
  • Vishisht Seva Medal
